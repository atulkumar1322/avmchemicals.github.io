About AVM Chemicals

Established in 1976, AVM Chemicals (V.K. Chemical Industries) has been a trusted name in the chemical manufacturing industry for decades. As a leading manufacturer, we specialize in producing high-quality chemicals, including acid slurry, finishing auxiliaries, commercial acids, industrial chemicals, and a wide range of specialty chemical solutions. With a strong commitment to innovation and quality, we continuously improve our manufacturing processes to meet evolving market demands. Our products are known for their reliability, ease of handling, and superior performance, making them ideal for industrial and commercial applications. At AVM Chemicals, we prioritize customer satisfaction by delivering genuine and trusted chemical solutions that align with international quality standards. Our dedication to excellence has enabled us to build long-term relationships with clients across various industries. Driven by expertise and a passion for chemical innovation, we strive to be at the forefront of the industry, ensuring that our products consistently meet the highest standards of efficiency and safety. Let us be your reliable partner in chemical manufacturing, providing solutions tailored to your needs.
